A sustainable 3D-printed business card with embedded NFC and a printed QR code. A tap or a scan opens your virtual contact card on the recipient's phone — no app needed, no paper wasted, no reprints when your details change. One card replaces a lifetime of printed stacks.

  • NFC enabled — tap on any modern phone to launch your vCard

Design my card
One 3D-printed business card, pre-programmed with your unique vCard URL on the NFC chip and printed QR code. We'll confirm the card design with you over email before printing.
Cards are printed in recyclable PLA — no solvents, no electroplating, no metal waste. The NFC chip is a standard NTAG 215 with 540 bytes of user memory. Total card weight ≈ 6g.
Free collection at KOE, IIUM. Standard shipping across West Malaysia is RM 5 flat. Expect 7-10 working days — we'll confirm the artwork with you before printing.
