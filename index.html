Why we exist

We make objects that outlast the moment they were made.

Durible began inside a workshop at IIUM's Kulliyyah of Engineering — a small bench, a 3D printer, a spool of warm red PLA. What started as keepsakes for alumni has grown into a quiet studio for everyday objects with a longer life: cards that update without reprinting, winders that outlive the cables, tags that carry a name for years.

Every object is printed to order, by hand, with recyclable PLA — not injection-molded overseas, not stamped out by the thousand. One considered object at a time.
