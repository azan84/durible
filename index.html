Ordo Lab · Durible™

Manufacturing meets Nature.

100% Locally Sourced Zero Petroleum Plastics Prototype Phase

Durible™ is Ordo's materials R&D initiative — developing bioplastics from durian skin, one of Malaysia's most abundant agricultural by-products. Instead of letting these husks become landfill, we're turning them into feedstock for a new generation of locally-sourced 3D printing materials.

Every object began inside a workshop at IIUM's Kulliyyah of Engineering — a small bench, a 3D printer, a commitment to making things that outlast the moment they were made. We started with keepsakes for alumni. Now we're building toward a future where the printer and the material are both born in Malaysia.
